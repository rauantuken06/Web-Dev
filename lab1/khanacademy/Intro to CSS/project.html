The BEST place where you can spend your holidays: Bora-Bora

Imagine turquoise water, soft white sand, and sunsets that look unreal. Bora Bora is a small island in
French Polynesia famous for its lagoon and overwater bungalows.

Spend your days snorkeling with colorful fish, cruising across the lagoon,
and enjoying fresh tropical fruit. This is the kind of place where every photo looks like a postcard.

What you will encouter

Things to see

  • The crystal-clear lagoon
  • Mount Otemanu (the dramatic volcanic peak)
  • Coral gardens and tropical marine life
  • Sunset views from the beach

Spots you must visit

  1. Lagoon tour by boat
  2. Matira Beach
  3. Local market for fruit and souvenirs
  4. Viewpoint hike for island panoramas
